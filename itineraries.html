One Day Trip

Jiufen Old Street and Shifen Waterfall

Step back in time with a visit to Jiufen Old Street, a charming hillside village known for its narrow alleys, traditional tea houses, and stunning views of the ocean. Afterward, head to nearby Shifen Waterfall, one of Taiwan's most beautiful natural wonders, where you can marvel at the cascading waters and snap some unforgettable photos.

Beitou Hot Springs and Tamsui Old Street

Indulge in a day of relaxation and rejuvenation with a visit to Beitou Hot Springs, a tranquil oasis located on the outskirts of Taipei. Soak in the healing waters of the hot springs, surrounded by lush greenery and scenic mountain views. Afterward, explore the charming streets of Tamsui Old Street, where you can stroll along the waterfront, sample local snacks, and admire historic architecture dating back to the Qing Dynasty.

Read More

Pingxi Sky Lantern and Houtong Cat Village

Experience the magin of the Pingxi Sky Lantern by writing your wishes on a lantern and sending it soaring into the heavens. Afterward, visit Houtong Cat Village, a charming community known for its friendly feline residents and picturesque scenery. Spend the afternoon wandering through the village streets, petting cats, and exploring local cafes and shops.

Musuem

National Palace Museum

Embark on a journey through the rich cultural heritage of China with a visit to the National Palace Museum in Taipei. Home to one of the world's most extensive collections of Chinese art and artifacts, this iconic museum offers visitors a captivating glimpse into the imperial history, artistic traditions, and cultural legacy of China.

Read More

Taipei Fine Arts Museum

Embark on a journey of artistic discovery at the Taipei Fine Arts Museum (TFAM), Taiwan's premier institution for modern and contemporary art. Located in the heart of Taipei's Xinyi District, TFAM showcases a diverse range of Taiwanese and international artworks across various mediums, offering visitors a captivating glimpse into the dynamic world of contemporary art.

Read More

Beitou Hot Springs Museum

Embark on a journey through time at the Beitou Hot Springs Museum, nestled in the picturesque Beitou District of Taipei. Discover the fascinating history, cultural significance, and therapeutic benefits of Taiwan's beloved hot springs as you explore this charming museum housed in a historic Japanese colonial-era building.

Old Street

Dihua Street

Located in Taipei's historic Dadaocheng district, Dihua Street is a bustling thoroughfare lined with historic shophouses, traditional stores and vibrant markets. Explore the street's eclectic mix of Chinese and Western architecture, browse through antique shops and herbal medicine stores, and sample local snacks like dried fruits, nuts, and candies.

Read More

Sanxia Old Street

Experience the charm of Taiwan's rural past at Sanxia Old Street, a quaint village known for its well-preserved historic buildings and traditional handicrafts. Wander through the cobblestone streets, admire the elegant architecture of the historic buildings, and shop for locally-made pottery, bamboo crafts, and handmade snacks.

Read More

Wanhua Old Street
(Herb Lane)

Discover the cultural melting pot of Wanhua Old Street, also known as Herb Lane, located in Taipei's bustling Wanhua District. Explore the street's vibrant mix of Chinese, Japanese, and Western influences, reflected in its historic buildings, temples, and street food stalls. Sample local delicacies like stinky tofu, oster omelets, and beef noodles, and browse through the area's eclectic shops, selling everything from traditional medicines and herbs to vintage clothing and accessories.

For Kids

Exploring Nature at Taipei Zoo and Maokong Gondola

Embark on a wild adventure at Taipei Zoo, one of the largest zoos in Asia, where kids can encouner a diverse array of animals from around the world including giant pandas, koalas, and African elephants. After exploring the zoo, take a scenic ride on the Maokong Gondola to Maokong, a tea-growing area known for its stunning views and traditional tea houses.

Read More

Learning Through Play at Taipei Children's Amusement Park

Let imaginations run wild at Taipei Children's Amusement Park, where kids can enjoy rides, games, and interactive exhibits that spark creativity and curiosity. From thrilling roller coasters and water slides to educational workshops and hands-on activities, there's something for every young adventure to enjoy.

Read More

Outdoor Fun at Daan Forest Park

Escape the city hustle and bustle and reconnct with nature at Daan Forest Park, Taipei's largest green space and urban oasis. Let kids run free on sparwling lawns, explore playgrounds and water features, and enjoy picnics amidst lush greenery.
