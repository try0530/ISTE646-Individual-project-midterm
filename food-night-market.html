Shilin Night Market

 Shilin Night Market is one of Taipei's largest and most iconic night markets, a must-visit destination for food enthusiasts and cultural explorers alike.
 At Shilin Night Market, food takes center stage, with countless stalls and vendors serving up a mouthwatering array of Taiwanese street food classics and international delicacies.

  • Shilin Night Market Information
    • Location: In the neighborhood of Dadong Rd., Danan Rd., Wenlin Rd. and Jihe Rd., Shilin Dist., Taipei City
    • Public Transportation:
      1. Take THSR (or TRA) to Taipei Station, transfer Taipei MRT to Jiantan Station
      2. Take the Taipei Sightseeing Bus Blue Route to the MRT Jiantain Station stop
  • Foods must eat in Shilin Night Market
    • Prince Cheese Potato
    • Hot Star - deep fried chicken cutlets
    • Zhong Cheng Hao - oyster omelets
    • Sheng Jian Bao
  • Things should try in Shilin Night Market
    • Catching fish
    • Shhoting games

Raohe Night Market

 At Raohe Night Market, food takes center stage, with a diverse array of stalls and vendors offering a tantalizing section of Taiwanese street food classics and innovative culinary.
 From sizzling barbecue skewers and aromatic grilled seafood to savory stinky tofu and mouthwatering desserts, there's something to tempt every palate and satify every craving.

  • Raohe Night Market Information
    • Location: located on Raohe Street and is about 600 meters long.
    • Public Transportation:
      1. Walking 100m (350ft) north of Exit 5 of the Songshan Station of the Taipei Metro
  • Foods must eat in Raohe Night Market
    • Black Peper Bun
    • Deep-Fried Potato Spiral
    • Stinky Tofu
    • Aiyu and Grass Jelly
    • Deep-Fried Mushrooms
    • Grilled Boar Sausage
    • Fried Milk on a Stick

Ningxia Night Market

 There are many traditional street food at Ningxia Night Market. More than just a food market, Ningxia Night Market is a cultural treasure trove that reflects the rich tapestry of Taiwanese life and traditions.

  • Ningxia Night Market Information
    • Location: No. 44, Ningxia Rd, Datong District, Taipei City, Taiwan
    • Public:
      1. Take MRT Red Line to Shunanglian Station and then take exit 1 or 2 west out of the station. The night market is about two blocks down the road.
  • Foods must eat in Ningxia Night Market
    • Liu Yu Zi - crispy taro balls and yolk taro cake
    • Rong's Pork Liver
    • Huan Ji Seasame Oil Chicken
    • Hong Ji Scallion Pancake Rolls
    • Beans Village
