Welcom To Taipei

Discovering Taipei's vibrant neighborhoods, iconic landmarks, and hidden gems is made easy with the city's efficient and convenient transportation system. From modern metro lines and extensive bus networks to affordable bike-sharing programs and convenient taxi services, getting around Taipei is a breeze for locals and visitors alike.

Passes to use in Taipei

  • EasyCard
    • EasyCard is a useful card to use in the Taipei transportation.
      You can use it in most of the transport systems, such as MRT, buses, and YouBike.
      And you can also use it to pay in many shops, such as convenient stores, Starbucks.
    • Where to get: Convenient Store, MRT Station
  • Taipei FunPass
    • Where to get: Online
  • One time ticket
    • Where to get: MRT Station
  • Cash

Taipei Metro (MRT)

The Taipei Metro, also known as the MRT, is the backbone of Taipei's transportation network, offering fast, reliable, and convenient travel throughout the city and beyond.

  • Opening Time: 6AM - Midnight
  • Passes: EasyCards, Single-journey Tokens, Taipei FunPass
  • Services: Attendants, Ticket Machines, Toilets

Taipei Bus System

Taipei's extensive bus system provides another convenient option for getting around the city. With a vast network of routes that crisscross the city and connect to remote areas and suburban districts, Taipei buses offer flexibility and accessibility for travelers looking to explore off-the-beaten-path destinations.

  • Opening Time:
    Most of the buses operate from 5:30 am - 11:30 pm
  • Passes: EasyCards, Taipei FunPass, Cash
  • App: Bus Traker Taipei | iOS | Android
  • Tips should know before taking a bus
    1. Wave hand when you see the bus you plan to take.
    2. Double check the number and color of the bus before you get on.

Taipei YouBike

Taipei's YouBike system offers a convenient and sustainable way to explore the city on two wheels. There are many stations around Taipei city that you can easily rent and return by youself.

  • Opening Time: 24hrs
  • Passes: EasyCard, Credit Cards, Cellphone
  • Price: NT$10 / 30 Minutes (0 - 4 hrs) | NT$20 / 30 Minutes (4 - 8 hrs) | NT$40 / 30 Minutes (8+ hrs)
  • How to rent:
    If you want to rent a YouBike, you'll need to register your EasyCard with a local phone number for verification and an ID number for insurance.
    You can also use a credit card in place of an EasyCard if you don't hold a local SIM.

Taipei Taxi

Hailing a yellow, metered taxi is easy except during rush hour and rain storms. You can easily get a taxi on the street or by call or APP.

  • Opening Time: 24hrs
  • How to get a taxi
    1. Get a car on the street by wave hand to the taxi
    2. Call: 55850 press 3 after phone on
    3. APP: LINE TAXI | 55688 | 173叫車
  • Tips should know before taking a bus
    1. Make sure the driver turns on the meter which begins at NT$70 for the first 0.77 miles and NT$5 for each additional 0.12 miles. A NT$20 surcharge is added to rides after 11p.m.
    2. Some taxis take credit cards, but ask before you ride begins
  • You can use Uber to get a drive too.
